About Us

PocketLawyers is a virtual law firm poised to
democratize access to premium legal solutions
for SMEs and Startups.


This is enabled by technology and intense storytelling and real life scenarios, as part of our core focus is to educate and re-educate SME and Startup owners on the absolute need for legal services. PocketLawyers was borne out of the need to provide access to simple and affordable legal documentation and advice to individuals and SMEs. We are in the business of ensuring legal and financial inclusion by simplifying legal processes, documentation and providing legal advice. The need for legal documentation in everyday life and business cannot be overemphasized. We are bridging the legal gap, increasing legal participation and fostering seamless business transactions in Nigeria and across Africa.

Our Vision

To become the platform of first choice for accessing legal documentation and advisory by SMEs, thereby bridging the legal gap, increasing legal participation and fostering seamless business transactions in Nigeria and across Africa.

We are strongly committed to promoting inclusion, breaking legal barriers and changing the age old legal narrative. We are about dispelling the notion that Lawyers are inaccessible and legal services are out of the financial reach of the average Nigerian and small/medium business owner. This is in key fulfillment of goal eight of the global goal for sustainable development to make sure everybody gets the benefit of entrepreneurship and innovation and goal sixteen access to justice and legal representation.
